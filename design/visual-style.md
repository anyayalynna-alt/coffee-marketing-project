# 🎨 Visual Style & Design Direction

## 📌 Overall concept
The visual identity of the brand is clean, modern, and эмоционально тепла.
The goal is to combine premium coffee aesthetics with a cozy lifestyle feeling.

---

## 🎨 Color palette
- Beige / cream tones – warmth and comfort
- Dark brown – coffee identity
- Black – minimalism and contrast
- Soft green – natural and organic feel

---

## 🔤 Typography
- Sans-serif fonts (clean and modern)
- Simple and readable
- Minimal use of decorative fonts

---

## 📸 Visual style

### Content type:
- Close-up coffee shots (espresso, cappuccino)
- Lifestyle photos (morning routine, люди з кавою)
- детали (зерна, текстури, процес приготування)

### Style:
- Natural lighting (сонячне світло)
- Soft shadows
- Minimalistic композиція
- Focus on texture and деталях

---

## 📱 Instagram feed style
- Alternating content:
  - Product
  - Lifestyle
  - Educational
- Consistent color tones
- No visual overload

---

## 🧠 Design inspiration

Inspired by:
- Scandinavian minimalism
- Specialty coffee бренди
- Cozy morning routines

---

## 💚 What makes this design strong
- Simple but memorable
- Emotional connection with audience
- Looks premium but accessible
- Easy to scale for different campaigns
