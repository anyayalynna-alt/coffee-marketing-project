## Foundation Coffee Roasters

### 📍 About
Ukrainian coffee brand founded in 2015, focused on quality coffee products, education, and широку лінійку товарів (зерно, дріпи, капсули, аксесуари). :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

### 📱 Content they post
- Product photos (coffee packs, new collections)
- Educational content (how to brew coffee, tips)
- Lifestyle visuals (morning coffee эстетика)
- Promotions and new product launches

### 💚 What I like
- Clean and aesthetic visual style
- Strong brand identity (colors, packaging)
- Mix of educational + lifestyle content
- Feels like a premium but friendly brand

## Mad Coffee (Mad Heads)

### 📍 About
Fast-growing specialty coffee brand from Kyiv with strong focus on unique beans and modern roasting techniques. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

### 📱 Content they post
- Specialty coffee storytelling (origin of beans)
- Experimental coffee (anaerobic, rare lots)
- Behind-the-scenes (roasting process)
- Minimalistic, trendy visuals

### 💚 What I like
- Modern and bold branding
- Focus on product uniqueness
- Feels more “trendy” and niche
- Appeals to younger audience

---

## Coffee Boutique

### 📍 About
Coffee shop / brand focused on premium coffee experience and атмосферу.

### 📱 Content they post
- Aesthetic coffee shots in cafés
- Interior and атмосферні фото
- Customer experience (people, lifestyle)
- Seasonal drinks and меню

### 💚 What I like
- Very Instagram-friendly visuals
- Cozy and emotional vibe
- Focus on experience, not just product
- Good use of lighting and композиції

| Brand           | Strength                     | Style                |

- All brands combine product + lifestyle content
- Visual style is very important in coffee niche
- Educational content builds trust
- Emotional content (vibe, atmosphere) increases engagement
